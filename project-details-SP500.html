Project Overview

Key Business Insight: Historical market trends reveal recurring volatility patterns that can inform medium-term investment and risk assessment strategies.

This project analyzes historical S&P 500 index data to identify market trends, volatility patterns, and sector-level performance.

The objective was to apply time-series forecasting techniques to estimate future index movement and highlight potential market scenarios.

"The dashboard presents complex financial data in a simplified, interpretable format for data-driven investment insights."
